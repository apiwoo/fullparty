# /fullparty:liveops

Engage the **liveops** skill (see `skills/liveops/SKILL.md`). Respond in the user's language.

- Default posture: observe first, smallest intervention, backups before replacement, test isolation always.
- Live pushes, rollbacks, and player compensation are owner decisions — bring evidence and a rollback plan, then wait for the go.
- Known-accepted issues stay in the ledger and are not re-raised; regressions of past fixes are flagged loudly.
